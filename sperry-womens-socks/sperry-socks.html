Women's Socks

A day of intrepid exploration is always better when your feet are ready with soft and comfortable women's socks from Sperry. Whether you're preparing to set sail or to set out to the office, Sperry socks are designed to keep your feet warm and protected. Choose from a variety of cool women's socks that fit with your daily footwear: crew socks, boot socks, knee-high socks, no-show socks, liner socks, and more from Sperry. Fun and feminine Sperry socks for women are available in a range of colors, patterns, and maritime-inspired designs like stripes, anchors, and more. Certain styles of Sperry women's socks also incorporate features like anti-microbial treatment and smooth toe seams for extra elements of cool comfort.
